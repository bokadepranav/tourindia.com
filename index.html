Planning a vacation in India? Looking for something useful to read about travelling? Have a look below.
Sri Venkateswara Swamy Temple, Andhra Pradesh

Venkateswara Temple is a Hindu temple situated in town of Tirumala in Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh, India.

The Sundarban, West Bengal

A national park, also popular as a tiger reserve in West Bengal. Situated at the delta formed by Ganga, Brahmaputra and Meghna rivers. Sundarban tour packages is best for wildlife lovers as well as leisure travellers.

Darjeeling, West Bengal

Darjeeling foundly called "Queen of Hills" , provides a perfect gateway for those seeking to be in harmony with nature. Have a look at Jaw-dropping locales, mesmerising sunrises, the untouched beauty of the hills here.

Chandigarh, Punjab

For nightlife, sightseeing tour, shopping, authentic Punjabi cuisine, festivals, architecture and design Chandigarh must be in your list.

Mysore Palace, Karnataka

If you have an eye for appreciating architectural designs then Mysore Palce is must visit for you. It is one of the things every tourist should try as it gives an interestin narration about the history of the palace and Royal family of Wodeyars.

Guwahati, Assam

Being one of the best tourist places in Assam, the city of Guwahati is a perfect blend of nature, culture, heritage, religion, and buzzing nightlife.

Khajuraho, Madhya Pradesh

The splendour of the intricate statues is one of the reasons that makes Khajuraho a popular site to visit among tourists.

Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Manali's is still one of the coolest places to visit during summer. No matter how many times you visit Manali, you cannot ignore the fact that it provides relief from the brutal brunt of summer in Indiaâ€™s plains.

Goa

It's no secret that Goa is a favourite beach destination in India. It has a laid-back lifestyle, hippie vibe, glorious scenic beaches, natural tranquillity, beach sports, and awesome parties.

Vrindavan, Uttar Pradesh

Vrindavan is a perfect tourism destination to lose yourself in the devotion of Krishna and to learn about his life.

Nainital, Uttarakhand

The Lake District of India - Nainital is one of the most charismatic hill stations that India harbors. Nainital bounded by green mountains make a really high quality picture.

Mumbai, Maharashtra

An abundance of historical and cultural sites makes Mumbai one of the country's most fascinating destinations.

Assam

Assam is a brimming tourist hub everything from the ancient temples, the wildlife sanctuaries, the scenic beauty of Brahmaputra and its unique culture.

Kedarnath Temple, Uttarakhand

A popular destination for hindu pilgrims, kedarnath is one of the four major sites in India's Chhota char Dham pilgrimage. Its best to enjoy the beauty of nature and waterfalls on the way to Kedarnath temple.

Zanskar, Himalaya

Zanskar is popular with adventure junkies. A hotspot for trekking, paragliding and river rafting, Lamayuru to Darcha and Lamayuru-Padum are some of the popular trekking trails that pass around Zanskar.

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

The land of Varanasi (Kashi) has been the ultimate pilgrimage spot for Hindus for ages. If you are seeking a place to gain experience of spiritual awekening, positive vibrations and peacful holiday then varanasi is best destination for your next trip.
